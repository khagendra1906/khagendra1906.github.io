About Me

KJ

Deputy Technical Officer at IIIT-Delhi

I am a passionate researcher and engineer with over 8 years of experience in wireless communications and emerging technologies. Currently pursuing my PhD at IIIT-Delhi while serving as Deputy Technical Officer in the ECE Department.

My research focuses on cutting-edge technologies including Reconfigurable Intelligent Surfaces, Visible Light Communication, and Device-to-Device Communication. I have published numerous papers in top-tier conferences and journals, and hold several patents in my research areas.

I am committed to bridging the gap between theoretical research and practical implementation, mentoring students, and contributing to the advancement of wireless communication technologies.
